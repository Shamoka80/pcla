Rooted in Faith, Focused on Growth

With two North Charleston locations, Poplar Christian Learning Academy supports young children through faith-centered care, age-appropriate learning, meaningful play, and warm relationships with trusted caregivers.

Program Highlights

Sunflowers — Infants

A calm, responsive infant environment designed for safety, connection, sensory discovery, and early developmental growth.

Little Sprouts — 1 Year Olds

A joyful toddler space where one-year-olds explore color, texture, movement, language, and social connection through guided play.

Rosebuds — 2 & 3 Year Olds

A supportive classroom for two- and three-year-olds focused on independence, communication, confidence, creativity, and school readiness.
