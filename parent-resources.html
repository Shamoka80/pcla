Preparing for Care

Starting childcare is an important step for both children and families. PCLA encourages families to ask questions, review classroom expectations, and contact the academy directly for guidance before enrollment.

  • Call to ask about current availability.
  • Ask which program best fits your child’s age and developmental stage.
  • Review any supply needs before the first day.
  • Ask about daily routines and classroom expectations.
  • Confirm the best next step for enrollment or tour information.

Common Parent Questions

How do I ask about enrollment?
Please call Poplar Christian Learning Academy at (843) 225-1004 for enrollment inquiries, current availability, program details, and tour information.
Where is PCLA located?
Poplar Christian Learning Academy serves families through two North Charleston locations: 1483 Remount Rd. and 2522 Nazarene Street.
What ages does PCLA serve?
PCLA provides early learning and childcare programs for young children, including infants, one-year-olds, two- and three-year-olds, and preschool-aged children. Families should call for current program availability.
Can I get enrollment details online?
For the most current enrollment details, families should call PCLA directly at (843) 225-1004.
How can I get the most current supply list or daily schedule?
Families may use the resources on this page when available and should call PCLA directly to confirm the most current information.
