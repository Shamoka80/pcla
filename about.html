A Heart for Families

Poplar Christian Learning Academy was founded from the Aiken family’s heart to serve other families. As working parents, Mr. and Mrs. Aiken understood how important it is to find a safe, nurturing place where children can be loved, cherished, respected, and educated while their parents work.

That heart for families continues to shape the academy’s mission today. PCLA provides a warm, faith-centered environment where young children are supported through meaningful relationships, age-appropriate learning, guided play, and consistent care.

What Defines PCLA

  • Family-centered care
  • Christian values
  • Safe and nurturing classrooms
  • Two North Charleston locations

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, faith-centered early learning environment where children are loved, respected, encouraged, and prepared to grow socially, emotionally, spiritually, physically, and academically.

Faith-Centered Care

PCLA reflects Christian values through love, patience, kindness, respect, and encouragement in daily care.

Safe, Nurturing Environment

Children are supported in classrooms designed to help them feel secure, comfortable, and ready to learn.

Development Through Play

Meaningful play helps children build language, creativity, confidence, motor skills, and social awareness.

Partnership with Families

PCLA values clear communication with families and encourages parents to call with questions about programs, enrollment, availability, and next steps.
